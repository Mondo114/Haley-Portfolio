About

I am an illustrator and animator from Long Beach California. I graduated from California State University Long Beach with a Bachelors degree in illustration and animation in 2016, since then I have been working to find my place in the art world. My passion is creating fun, whimsical pieces of art.

Whether I’m working with traditional media such as colored pencil and gouache or painting digitally, I strive to create endearing characters and compelling narratives. I love working with clients to create beautiful pieces, and I’m always looking for new people to collaborate with. I am also interested in full time art opportunities.
